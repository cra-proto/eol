Prepare tax returns for someone who died

When someone dies, their representative must file their final tax returns for any income they received in the year of death, including on the value of any assets they own (deemed disposition of property and belongings).

If there is income after the death, a T3 trust return might also be required to report income of the estate.

You may also choose to file optional returns for specific types of income to reduce or eliminate tax that you would otherwise have to pay for the person who died.
