Income from the year of death

Income in the final tax year is handled differently depending on whether it was received before or after the date of death.

On this page

  • Determine if income was received before or after the death
  • Types of income

Determine if income was received before or after the death

Before the date of death: Option 1 of 2

Personal income tax return

Income received before the date of death must be included as part of the final income tax return for the deceased.

After the date of death: Option 2 of 2

Eligible for optional return: rights or things

If the person who died received income after the date of death, you may be eligible to file an optional return for rights or things which generally will allow you to claim a larger credit on the estate's taxes.

Alternatively, you can claim income received after the date of death as part of the basic personal income tax return for the deceased.
